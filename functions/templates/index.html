What is Data Analysis?

Data analysis is the process of collecting, organizing, and interpreting data to find patterns, trends, and insights. Data analysis can help you make better decisions, solve problems, and improve performance.

Upload Your Data File

Upload your data file in CSV format and get instant insights.

How to Upload Your Data File

Follow these steps to upload your data file:

  • Prepare your data in a spreadsheet software, such as Excel, Google Sheets, or LibreOffice Calc.
  • Save your data as a CSV file. A CSV file is a plain text file that contains comma-separated values.
  • Click on the "Choose CSV File" button and select your CSV file from your computer.
  • Click on the "Analyze Data" button and wait for the results.
